Whether riding shotgun with Jack Kerouac in On the Road, exploring the rooms of the suggestively Chilean city house in Isabel Allendeâ€™s The House of the Spirits, sitting in the cockpit with the Fly Boys in World War II, setting eyes on Pemberley for the first time with Elizabeth in Pride and Prejudice, or racing through the street's of Dante's Florence with Robert Langdon in Dan Brown's Inferno, we have all felt the power of books to let us experience another life set in a world different from our own. Yet we often forget that the worlds of stories overlap with the real world.

Booma: The Bookmapping Project is providing a platform for readers everywhere to map the worlds of books, both fiction and nonfiction, onto an interactive map of the real world.

If you have ever wanted to find out where the events of your favorite book take place, if you have ever wanted to know the literary history of the places you have been, if you just love books or traveling or both, Booma is the place for you.

Communities

Want to build a fun and engaging project around books and literature by bringing a mapping event to your community? We can help you energize your community to produce something they can share with pride.

Educators

Want to help students engage with their reading and visualize where the events in books take place? Want to provide a project that supports close reading, research, writing for publication, and more?
