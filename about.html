Music by The Secession.

Whether riding shotgun with Jack Kerouac in On the Road, exploring the rooms of the suggestively Chilean city house in Isabel Allende's The House of the Spirits, sitting in the cockpit with the Fly Boys in World War II, setting eyes on Pemberley for the first time with Elizabeth in Pride and Prejudice, or racing through the street's of Dante's Florence with Robert Langdon in Dan Brown's Inferno, we have all felt the power of books to let us experience another life set in a world different from our own. Yet we often forget that the worlds of stories overlap with the real world.

Booma: The Bookmapping Project is providing a platform for readers everywhere to map the worlds of books, both fiction and nonfiction, onto an interactive map of the real world.

Build

Making a project like Booma work relies on having a strong foundation. If you have skills in front end or back end development, we would love you to contribute on our GitHub page.

Have an idea to make the site better? Suggest a feature: booma+suggestion@booma.us.

Contribute

As the Booma platform develops, we will be making it easier to contribute to existing projects and even create new ones. For now sign up to help with one of our upcoming projects or recommend something new: booma+project@booma.us.

Follow us on Facebook for more updates.

Donate

So far, we've survived by learning how to do things for ourselves and relying on the contributions and advice of friends. We'd love to do even more.

If you like what we're doing, consider making a recurring donation through our Patreon page and help us keep doing it.
